Lamborghini is a legendary Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini. Here are some key details about Lamborghini: - Headquartered in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy - Known for their sleek, futuristic designs and high-performance vehicles - Iconic models include the Miura, Countach, Diablo, Murciélago, Gallardo, Aventador, and Huracán - V12 engines are a signature feature of Lamborghini cars - Founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini, a successful entrepreneur and Ferrari owner who wanted to create his own high-performance cars - Acquired by Volkswagen Group in 1998 - Continues to produce exclusive, cutting-edge supercars that embody the brand's philosophy of innovation, style, and performance.

Porsche



Porsche is a renowned German luxury sports car manufacturer founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche. Here are some key details about Porsche: - Headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany - Known for their high-performance, precision-engineered vehicles - Iconic models include the 911, Boxster, Cayman, Panamera, Macan, and Cayenne - Famous for their distinctive design, rear-engine layout (in some models), and exceptional handling - Founded by Ferdinand Porsche, an Austrian engineer who also designed the Volkswagen Beetle - Acquired by Volkswagen Group in 2012 - Continues to produce high-performance, luxury cars that embody the brand's philosophy of performance, innovation, and style Some interesting facts about Porsche include: - The company's logo is based on the coat of arms of the Free People's State of Württemberg - Porsche cars have won numerous races, including the 24 Hours of Le Mans - The 911 is one of the most iconic and enduring sports car designs in history

Rolls Royce
